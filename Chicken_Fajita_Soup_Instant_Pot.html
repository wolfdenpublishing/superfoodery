Chicken Fajita Soup (Instant Pot)

All the charred, smoky flavors of restaurant fajitas in a hearty soup. The Instant Pot saut√© function caramelizes the peppers and onions for authentic fajita taste, while pressure cooking melds the flavors and creates fall-apart tender chicken.

Ingredients

For the Chicken

  • 1.5 lb boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 tbsp avocado oil
  • Salt and pepper

For the Fajita Vegetables

  • 2 tbsp avocado oil
  • 2 bell peppers (red and yellow/orange), sliced
  • 1 large yellow onion, sliced into half-moons
  • 1 poblano pepper, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced

Spice Blend

  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • ¬Ω tsp oregano
  • ¬Ω tsp black pepper
  • ¬º tsp habanero powder (adjust to taste)

Liquid & Body

  • 4 cups chicken bone broth
  • 1 can (14 oz) fire-roasted diced tomatoes
  • 2 limes, juiced (divided)

For Serving

  • Fresh cilantro, roughly chopped
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • Lime wedges
  • Cotija cheese, crumbled (optional)

Instructions

1. Sear the Chicken
  1. Set Instant Pot to Saut√© mode (high). Add 1 tablespoon avocado oil and let heat for 1 minute.
  2. Season chicken thighs generously with salt and pepper on both sides.
  3. Sear chicken 2-3 minutes per side until golden brown. Don't cook through‚Äîjust building flavor. Remove and set aside.
2. Char the Vegetables (This Is the Secret!)
  1. Add remaining 2 tablespoons avocado oil to the pot.
  2. Add bell peppers, onion, and poblano in a single layer as much as possible. Let them sit undisturbed for 2-3 minutes to develop char marks.
  3. Stir, then let sit again for another 2-3 minutes. Repeat until vegetables have nice color and slightly softened edges, about 6-8 minutes total.
  4. Add minced garlic and cook for 30 seconds until fragrant.
3. Build the Soup
  1. Add cumin, smoked paprika, chili powder, oregano, black pepper, and habanero powder. Stir for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  2. Pour in bone broth, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom of the pot (this is flavor gold!).
  3. Add fire-roasted tomatoes and juice of 1 lime. Stir to combine.
  4. Nestle chicken thighs back into the pot, submerging them in the liquid.
4. Pressure Cook
  1. Lock the lid and set the valve to sealing position.
  2. Pressure cook on HIGH for 12 minutes.
  3. Allow natural pressure release for 10 minutes, then quick release any remaining pressure.
5. Finish and Serve
  1. Remove chicken thighs and shred with two forks. Return shredded chicken to the pot.
  2. Stir in remaining lime juice. Taste and adjust salt as needed.
  3. Ladle into bowls and top generously with fresh cilantro, avocado slices, a lime wedge, and cotija cheese if using.
