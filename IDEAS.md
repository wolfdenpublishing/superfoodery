```markdown

```

When an idea is developed into a full recipe:
1. Remove the entry from this document

3. Update index.html

## Desserts

### Grilled/Roasted Peaches
Halved peaches roasted or air-fried until caramelized, topped with Greek yogurt, walnuts, and a drizzle of honey. The natural sugars concentrate beautifully with heat.
- **Key ingredients:** peaches, Greek yogurt, walnuts, honey, cinnamon
- **Notes:** Can use air fryer or oven. Could add a pinch of habanero for a unique twist.

### Berry Crumble
Mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries) baked with an oat-walnut-coconut oil crumble topping. Warm, comforting, and packed with antioxidants.
- **Key ingredients:** mixed berries, oats, walnuts, coconut oil
- **Notes:** Oats provide beta-glucan fiber. Can be made in individual ramekins for portion control.

### Dark Chocolate Avocado Mousse
Ripe avocado blended with cocoa powder, a touch of maple syrup or honey, and vanilla. Surprisingly decadent with healthy fats.
- **Key ingredients:** avocado, cocoa powder, honey/maple syrup, vanilla
- **Notes:** Creamy texture comes entirely from avocado. Can top with berries or cacao nibs.

### Frozen Banana Nice Cream
Frozen bananas blended until creamy like soft-serve ice cream. Naturally sweet with no added sugar needed.
- **Key ingredients:** frozen bananas
- **Notes:** Add-in options: cocoa powder, berries, nut butter, cinnamon. Bananas provide potassium and prebiotics.

### Mexican Chocolate Pudding
Rich chocolate pudding with warm spices - cinnamon, a hint of cayenne or habanero. Bold and satisfying.
- **Key ingredients:** cocoa powder, cinnamon, cayenne/habanero, milk/cream base
- **Notes:** Plays to the love of spicy + chocolate combination. Could use avocado or Greek yogurt as base for added nutrition.

### Stuffed Medjool Dates
Medjool dates stuffed with almond or walnut butter, topped with a walnut piece, optional dark chocolate drizzle. Intensely sweet naturally.
- **Key ingredients:** Medjool dates, nut butter, walnuts, dark chocolate
- **Notes:** Dates are high in fiber and minerals. Very rich - 2-3 dates is a satisfying portion.
