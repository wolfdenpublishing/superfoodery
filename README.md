## What Is This?

A curated collection of recipes built around **evidence-based superfoods** — foods with documented health benefits backed by nutritional science. No fads, no gimmicks, just real food that tastes incredible and fuels your body.

Every recipe is designed for:

- **Bold, authentic flavors** — healthy food shouldn't taste like punishment
- **Practical meal prep** — make once, eat well all week
- **OMAD compatibility** — optimized for one-meal-a-day eating patterns
- **Complete nutrition tracking** — calories, protein, fat, carbs, and fiber for every recipe

---

## The Superfoods Philosophy

These recipes prioritize ingredients with proven health benefits:

| Category | Superfoods |
|----------|-----------|
| **Omega-3 Rich** | Salmon, walnuts, chia seeds, flaxseeds |
| **Cruciferous** | Broccoli, cauliflower, Brussels sprouts, cabbage |
| **Anti-inflammatory** | Turmeric, ginger, garlic, olive oil |
| **Antioxidant-packed** | Blueberries, strawberries, dark chocolate |
| **Gut-friendly** | Greek yogurt, fermented foods, fiber-rich vegetables |

## Tech Notes

Recipes are stored as standalone HTML files with embedded [JSON-LD structured data](https://schema.org/Recipe), making them compatible with recipe management apps and search engines. The site is hosted via GitHub Pages.
