Superfood Chicken Salad

Chilled cubed Instant Pot chicken with red grapes, blueberries, and apple in a creamy Greek yogurt-turmeric dressing, topped with walnuts, pecans, hemp seeds, and fresh parsley. A superfood powerhouse hitting omega-3s, probiotics, anti-inflammatory compounds, and antioxidants in every bite.

Superfood High-Protein Omega-3 Anti-Inflammatory Meal Prep Instant Pot
Prep: 20 min Cook: 18 min (8 min pressure + 10 min NPR) Chill: 30 min - 2 hr Total: ~1 hr Servings: 4

Ingredients

Chicken

  • 1 lb frozen boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 2 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Salad

  • 1 cup red seedless grapes, halved
  • 1/2 cup fresh blueberries
  • 1 medium apple, cored and diced
  • 3/4 cup celery, diced (2-3 stalks)
  • 3 tbsp red onion, finely diced
  • 2 tbsp chopped walnuts
  • 2 tbsp chopped pecans
  • 2 tbsp fresh flat-leaf parsley, chopped, plus extra for garnish

Dressing

  • 3/4 cup plain full-fat Greek yogurt
  • 1 lemon, juiced (about 2 tbsp)
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp salt, adjust to taste
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil

Serving

  • 4 cups mixed greens or baby romaine
  • 1 tbsp hemp seeds
  • Extra virgin olive oil for finishing drizzle

Instructions

Cook the Chicken
  1. Add chicken broth, smashed garlic, salt, and pepper to the Instant Pot.
  2. Place frozen chicken thighs in the liquid — it's okay if they overlap.
  3. Pressure cook on HIGH for 8 minutes, then allow a 10-minute natural pressure release (NPR). Quick release any remaining pressure.
  4. Remove chicken to a cutting board. Let rest 5 minutes, then cut into 1/2-inch to 3/4-inch cubes. Discard garlic. (Save that cooking liquid — it's essentially a light chicken broth, great for soups.)
  5. Refrigerate cubed chicken while you prep everything else, or let it cool to room temperature at minimum.
Make the Dressing
  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine Greek yogurt, lemon juice, chia seeds, turmeric, black pepper, salt, and olive oil. Whisk until smooth and evenly golden from the turmeric.
  2. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes while you prep the other ingredients — the chia seeds will begin to hydrate and help thicken the dressing.
Assemble
  1. Add the chilled cubed chicken, grapes, blueberries, apple, celery, red onion, and parsley to the bowl with the dressing.
  2. Fold everything together gently — you don't want to crush the blueberries.
  3. Taste and adjust salt, pepper, or lemon juice as needed.
  4. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to let flavors meld (1-2 hours is even better). The chia seeds will fully hydrate and the dressing will thicken beautifully.
Serve
  1. Place 1 cup of greens on each plate or in each meal prep container.
  2. Spoon chicken salad over the greens.
  3. Sprinkle with extra parsley, hemp seeds, and a light drizzle of EVOO.

Notes

Meal Prep: Stores well for 4 days refrigerated. Keep greens separate from the chicken salad until serving to prevent wilting. The salad actually gets better on days 2-3 as the chia seeds fully hydrate and the dressing thickens. If it seems too thick after a day or two, stir in a teaspoon of lemon juice or water to loosen.

Calorie Guidance: At ~379 cal per serving, this works well as a component of your OMAD meal or as a substantial snack. Two servings (~758 cal) makes a solid main meal during fat loss phase with room for a small snack later.

Spicy Variation: Add a pinch of habanero powder to the dressing. The heat against the sweet fruit and creamy yogurt is incredible.

Peach Season Swap: When peaches are in season, swap the apple for a ripe diced peach.

Extra Crunch: Toast the walnuts and pecans in a dry skillet for 3-4 minutes before adding.

Superfood Highlights: This recipe checks omega-3s (walnuts, chia seeds, hemp seeds), alliums (red onion, garlic), berries (blueberries), quality protein, healthy fats (EVOO, walnuts), fermented food (yogurt), anti-inflammatory compounds (turmeric + black pepper), and fresh herbs (parsley).
