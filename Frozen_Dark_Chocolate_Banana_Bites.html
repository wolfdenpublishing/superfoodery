Frozen Dark Chocolate Banana Bites

Banana slices enrobed in dark bittersweet chocolate softened with coconut oil, frozen into perfectly portioned bites. A satisfying ice cream replacement packed with antioxidants from dark chocolate and potassium from banana. The coconut oil keeps the chocolate from freezing rock-hard, giving you a satisfying snap instead of a jawbreaker.

Ingredients

  • 4 oz dark bittersweet baking chocolate (60%+ cacao)
  • 4 tsp coconut oil
  • 1 medium banana

Optional Toppings

  • Flaked sea salt
  • Crushed walnuts
  • Habanero powder

Instructions

Prepare the Chocolate
  1. Break the chocolate bar into pieces and place in a microwave-safe bowl with the coconut oil.
  2. Microwave in 30-second bursts, stirring between each, until fully melted and smooth (usually 2-3 rounds).
Prepare the Banana
  1. Slice the banana into approximately 1/3-inch (8mm) rounds. You need 8 slices.
  2. Pat the banana slices dry with a paper towel to remove excess moisture. This helps the chocolate adhere and prevents it from seizing.
Fill the Molds
  1. Spoon a thin layer of melted chocolate into each of 8 silicone mold cavities, enough to cover the bottom.
  2. Place one banana slice into each cavity, pressing gently into the chocolate.
  3. Spoon remaining chocolate over each banana slice, covering completely.
  4. Optional: sprinkle with flaked sea salt, crushed walnuts, or a pinch of habanero powder before the chocolate sets.
Freeze and Store
  1. Place the mold in the freezer for at least 2 hours until completely solid.
  2. Pop the bites out of the mold and transfer to a freezer bag or airtight container. Store in the freezer for up to 2 months.

Notes

Scaling: This recipe is based on one 4 oz bar of chocolate. Scale up easily: 2 bars = 16 bites, 3 bars = 24 bites (fills a full 24-cavity mold). Use AnyList's built-in scaling to adjust.

Why coconut oil? Pure dark chocolate freezes rock-hard. The coconut oil keeps the coating at a satisfying snap rather than tooth-cracking brittle. The ratio is about 1 tsp coconut oil per 1 oz of chocolate.

Fat loss portioning: At ~99 calories per bite, 2-3 bites (198-297 cal) makes a satisfying snack within your daily budget. A full batch of 8 gives you 3-4 days of portions.

Chocolate: 70%+ cacao is ideal for maximum antioxidant benefit. 60% works well too â€” you'll just have slightly more sugar per bite.

Topping ideas: Flaked sea salt is the classic. Crushed walnuts add omega-3s and crunch. A tiny pinch of habanero powder on each bite gives a Mexican hot chocolate vibe.
