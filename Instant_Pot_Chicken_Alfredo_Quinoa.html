Instant Pot Chicken Alfredo with Quinoa

Rich, creamy alfredo sauce with shredded Instant Pot chicken over savory quinoa cooked in seasoned chicken juices. Fresh spinach, ginger, and habanero powder add superfood benefits and a warming kick. A satisfying OMAD meal with 53g of protein per serving.

Ingredients

Instant Pot Chicken

  • 3 lbs boneless skinless chicken thighs, frozen
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder

Quinoa (Instant Pot)

  • 2 cups quinoa, rinsed
  • Cooking liquid from chicken, plus water to reach 4 cups total
  • 2 tbsp jalapeno brine or pickle juice
  • 1 tbsp infused vinegar
  • Pinch of salt

Alfredo Sauce

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) butter
  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 8 oz cream cheese
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 cups grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 tbsp fresh ginger, finely grated
  • 1/4 to 1/2 tsp habanero powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika

Add-Ins

  • 6 oz fresh baby spinach

Instructions

Cook the Chicken (Instant Pot)
  1. Place frozen chicken thighs in the Instant Pot with 1 cup water, 1/2 tsp salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper, and 1/2 tsp garlic powder.
  2. Pressure cook on HIGH for 15 minutes. Natural release 10 minutes, then quick release remaining pressure. Add 3-5 minutes if thighs are frozen together in a solid block.
  3. Remove chicken and shred with two forks. Set aside.
Cook the Quinoa (Instant Pot)
  1. Keep the cooking liquid in the Instant Pot. Add water, 2 tbsp jalapeno brine or pickle juice, and 1 tbsp infused vinegar to reach 4 cups total liquid. Add a pinch of salt.
  2. Use Saute mode to bring the liquid to a boil. Stir in rinsed quinoa, turn off the Instant Pot, cover, and let sit 15-20 minutes until liquid is absorbed.
  3. Fluff quinoa with a fork.
Make the Alfredo Sauce (Stovetop, while quinoa rests)
  1. Whisk butter, heavy cream, and cream cheese in a large saucepan over medium heat until melted and smooth.
  2. Add minced garlic, garlic powder, Italian seasoning, salt, pepper, grated ginger, habanero powder, and smoked paprika. Whisk until smooth.
  3. Add grated parmesan cheese. Bring to a simmer and cook 5 minutes, stirring frequently.
Combine and Meal Prep
  1. Fold shredded chicken into the alfredo sauce. Add spinach in handfuls, stirring until wilted, about 2-3 minutes.
  2. Portion 3/4 cup quinoa into each of 8 meal prep containers. Top with chicken alfredo mixture. Refrigerate up to 5 days.

Notes

  • OMAD Fit: At ~1,023 calories with 53g protein, this works well as your main OMAD meal during fat loss phase, leaving ~477 calories for a small snack (Greek yogurt with berries, dark chocolate, etc.).
  • Lighter Portions: Can stretch to 10 servings for ~820 calorie portions.
  • Heat Level: Start with 1/4 tsp habanero powder and taste before going higher. The cream carries capsaicin differently than a broth-based sauce.
  • Ginger: At this ratio the ginger adds a subtle warm background note and digestive support without tasting identifiably like ginger.
  • Quinoa Flavor: The jalapeno brine/pickle juice and vinegar add a bright tang to the quinoa that cuts through the richness of the alfredo. Adjust amounts to taste.
  • Meal Prep: Refrigerate up to 5 days. Reheat in microwave. The alfredo will thicken when cold; it loosens back up when reheated.
